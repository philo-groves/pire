# Write-Up

Write for another analyst who was not present during the session.

Include:
- Scope and objective
- Confirmed findings
- Key evidence and artifacts
- Exact reproduction steps when they exist
- Open questions and rejected hypotheses that still matter

Rules:
- Separate facts from interpretations
- Preserve exact commands, hashes, symbols, offsets, and crash signatures
- Do not inflate confidence; label tentative conclusions clearly
- Prefer terse technical prose over narrative filler
